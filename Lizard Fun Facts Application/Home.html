Kids Facts About Lizards

Bearded dragon

Bearded dragonGrowing between 40 and 60cm in length (including the tail), the recommended size for a bearded dragon’s enclosure is at least 100cm long x 50cm wide x 60cm high. Visit your local Petbarn where a trained team member will be happy to recommend the right housing for your dragon. More information found at https://www.petbarn.com.au/petspot/reptile/reptile-right

Bluetongue Lizards

Blue Tounge Lizard

Blue-tongues can grow up to 60 cm in length. They have silvery grey to brown smooth scales, with distinct dark stripes running across their body and tail.

Where Are We Found

Blue-tongue lizards are found in just about every part of Australia: on the coastal plains; in the mountains; in rainforest; deserts and at least one species is found in every Australian capital city..

Yellow Geko

One of their most famous talents is their ability to scurry along slick surfaces — even glass windows or across ceilings. The only surface that geckos can't stick to is Teflon. Well, dry Teflon. Add water, however, and geckos can stick even to this seemingly impossible surface! They do this through specialized toe pads.

Chameleon

Habits. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches. Their hands and feet have large toes that help them cling to branches. Besides changing skin color, chameleons have another feature that no other animals have.

Goanna

The size of goanna depends on the species. On average, goanna can reach 4.6 feet in length and 13 pounds in weight.

How Awsome Are Goannas

Goanna

A body of goanna is covered with scales that can be green, black or brown, depending on the habitat. Desert species are usually brightly colored (yellow to red).
